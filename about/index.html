About me??

I am an epidemiologist and biostatistician who studies a variety infectious diseases. I love R and primarily use it for all of my research (although I do use python sometimes).

I'm currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center in the Schiffer Lab working on several projects. My current project focuses analyzing primary infection of CMV in infants. I'm also analyzing household shedding rates and transmission of several herpesviruses: CMV, EBV, HSV, and HHV6. I have also studied the kinetics of bacteria in the vaginal microbiota during bacterial vaginosis.


Though I currently reside in Seattle, I carry a lot of hometown pride for my homeland of Michigan.
